ENCEBOLLADO

DESCRIPTION

Encebollado is a fish broth that contains albacore, cassava, tomato, red onion, cilantro, chili powder, cumin and other spices.

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 Tablespoon Vegetable Oil
  • 1 1/2 Cups Red Onion, chopped into thin strips
  • 3 ripe kidney tomatoes, quartered
  • 1/2 Tablespoon Ground Cumin
  • 2 teaspoons dry chili pepper
  • 1 1/2 Liter Water
  • 4 Tablespoons Finely chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1 pound fresh albacore, sliced
  • 2 Cups Yucca cut into cubes
  • 2/3 Cup Red Pepper, chopped into large pieces
  • 2/3 Cup Green Pepper Chopped into large pieces
  • 1/2 Cup Celery, coarsely chopped
  • 2 Maggi Vegetable Broth Cubes
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 4 Units Lemon cut into quarters

Steps to follow:

  1. Place the water in a pot to boil, add 2 tomatoes and half a chopped onion, add the celery, the pepper pieces, 2 tablespoons of cilantro, garlic, cumin, salt, pepper and dried chili.
  2. In another separate pot, cook the previously chopped cassava with plenty of water until it is soft.
  3. Separately, make the onion pickle with the rest of the finely chopped Paite√±a onion, washed, drained and seasoned with salt, lemon and the remaining chopped cilantro. Let it rest until it is cured
  4. Add the albacore loins to the broth from step 1, cook it completely and remove from the pot.
  5. After the cooked albacore has been reserved, remove the vegetables from the broth and place them in a blender, add a little cooked yucca and a little yuca cooking liquid. Sift the processed liquid over the broth and add the yuca cooking water. Dissolve the MAGGI Vegetable Broth cubes. Bring to a boil for 5 minutes over medium heat. We return the albacore to the preparation.
  6. Adjust the flavors with salt and pepper. Place some soup with pieces of yucca and albacore on the plate and serve accompanied with a little of the onion curtido that you prepared previously.
